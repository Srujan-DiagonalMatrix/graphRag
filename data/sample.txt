The Matrix is a science fiction film about a simulated reality where humans are unknowingly trapped. Neo discovers the truth and joins a rebellion against machines. The story explores artificial reality and perception.

Keanu Reeves is an actor known for roles in The Matrix, John Wick, and Speed. His characters often face high-stakes action and moral decisions.

John Wick is an action thriller about a retired hitman forced back into violence after a personal loss. The film is known for its stylized action and underground criminal world.

Speed is an action film where a city bus is rigged with a bomb that will detonate if the bus slows down too much. Sandra Bullock and Keanu Reeves must keep the passengers safe.

Los Angeles is a major city in California that appears as a setting in many action films. It is often used for urban chase scenes and crime stories.
